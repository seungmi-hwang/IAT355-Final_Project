Project Focus
Removable cover + expandable prep deck

Simplifying life with the soft forms

Our project explores a reversible, removable stovetop cover designed for two-coil induction stoves. The goal is to add prep space and integrated storage for renters living in small apartments—without modifying the kitchen.
