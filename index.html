An expandable prep surface for compact rental kitchens

Slide & Store is a renter-friendly induction cover that transforms unused cooktop space into an expandable prep area. The design combines sliding and roll-out interactions with integrated storage, enabling flexible use in compact kitchens without permanent modification.
